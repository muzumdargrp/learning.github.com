About Us

Building AI-native systems that can be trusted.

Learning Semantics is an AI-native research and infrastructure company focused on governed, agent-based intelligence systems designed for real-world deployment.

Our Mission

To enable meaningful use of AI in environments where accountability matters — by embedding governance, human authority, and auditability directly into system design.

Our Approach

AI-Native by Design

AI agents are not features — they are the execution layer of the system.

Human-Governed

Humans retain semantic authority, approval control, and accountability.

Governance as Architecture

Auditability, versioning, and review are built into the system — not added later.

Governance & Research Roles

Learning Semantics maintains a lean human governance structure, with AI agents performing the majority of research and execution operations.
